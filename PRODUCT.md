# MODE — Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

MODE is a trainer-aware AI fitness coaching app. It connects clients with personalized AI coaching — daily check-ins, training recommendations, chat with a coach — and gives personal trainers a platform to manage clients, capture their training knowledge, and let their AI assistant handle routine communication.

Every screen earns attention by being immediately useful. No vanity UI.

## Users

**Clients** — fitness-focused individuals tracking training, completing daily check-ins, and chatting with their AI coach. They use MODE on their phone at the gym, at home, or commuting. Ambient light varies. They want clarity fast: what's today's move, how am I doing, what does my coach say. Critically: every client is coached through their assigned trainer's lens — the AI reflects that trainer's style, knowledge, and program. Client-facing screens should feel personal and specific, never generic.

**Personal trainers** — independent coaches managing a roster of clients. They use MODE to monitor client progress, capture training knowledge, review AI-generated coach responses, and run their assistant flows. They're often in a gym environment or at a desk reviewing sessions.

## Brand Tone

Premium, focused, human.

Not: clinical, medical-app white-and-teal.
Not: gamified, aggressive, Whoop-dark-plus-neon.
Not: generic SaaS navy-and-purple.
Not: Nike-campaign maximalism.

The product feels like a serious tool made for people who take their training seriously. Warm confidence, not hype.

## Anti-References

- Generic health app pastels and rounded-everything
- Whoop's neon-on-black aggression
- Nike campaign-style maximalism
- White-and-teal wellness cleanliness
- Purple gradient on white (SaaS default)
- Fitness app gamification badges and confetti

## Strategic Principles

- Every screen earns attention by being immediately useful.
- One clear primary action per screen.
- Calm, low-cognitive-load for check-in and onboarding flows.
- Data-dense but legible for trainer-platform surfaces.
- The MODE state system (build / beast / recover / rest) is the personality layer — surfaces adapt to it when appropriate.
